How it works

From "where am I going?" to "tie this on."

Built for the traveling angler — the work trip, the family vacation, the new lake down the road.

Pick a place

Click any state, ecoregion, or named water on the map — or pick a species you love and see where it lives.

See what's there

The species list filters to that location. Flip on Angling mode to rank what's actually biting by month and time of day.

Know where to fish

Real destination waters drop as pins — each with the best season, how to fish it, top baits, access, and nearest town.

What's inside

A field guide that actually knows the water.

🗺️

Ecoregion-precise ranges

US freshwater fish are mapped to EPA Level III ecoregions — so a brook trout lights up the Blue Ridge, not the whole state.

📍

Named destination waters

188 real lakes, rivers, tailwaters, marshes and bluewater spots — with season, tactics, baits, access and size for each.

🎣

"What's biting now"

One tap sets the current month and time and ranks every species by how likely it is to bite right now.

🌊

Inshore to bluewater

Saltwater species sit in the right water — flats and surf inshore, tuna and sailfish offshore, with depth and distance.

📋

Official regulations

Every state links straight to its wildlife agency's current rules and license page — no guessing, no stale limits.

📷

Photos & field data

Real photos, world records, diet, fight rating, water temp and tactics for all 56 species.
